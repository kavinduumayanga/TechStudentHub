Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit this website and how it may be used.

Overview

Tech & Student Help Hub (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a static informational website. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. This policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the site and how it may be used.

Information we may collect

1) Information you provide

If you contact us via email or a mailto form, you may provide personal information such as your email address and the content of your message. We use that information only to respond and maintain basic correspondence.

2) Log and device information

Like most websites, hosting providers may automatically collect basic logs (for example: IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and timestamps) to operate and secure the service. These logs are typically controlled by your hosting provider.

3) Cookies and similar technologies

This site may use cookies if third-party services are enabled (for example, advertising). Cookies can help with ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. If you do not want cookies, you can adjust your browser settings.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

If you enable Google AdSense or other advertising services, those services may collect or receive information about your visits to this and other websites to provide ads. Ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies.

We do not control how third-party ad networks operate. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party services you enable.

How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the website
  • To respond to messages sent via email
  • To understand aggregated site performance (if analytics are enabled)
  • To display ads (only if advertising is enabled)

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers you choose to use (hosting, advertising, analytics)
  • If required to comply with applicable laws or lawful requests
  • To protect the security and integrity of the site

Data retention

Email messages may be retained for as long as necessary to respond and keep basic records. Hosting logs are retained according to the hosting provider’s policies.

Children’s privacy

This site is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so it can be removed where feasible.

Links to other sites

Our articles may link to external resources. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party sites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will be revised when changes are made.

Contact
